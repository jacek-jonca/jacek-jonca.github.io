ABOUT ME

I am a Texas based consultant, leader, and a fan of crunching data. I help organizations grow and help people live happier, healthier, and more fulfilling lives. I established and lead the Graduate Center and the Office of Postdoctoral Affairs at Texas Tech University. I hold master’s degree in Social Psychology and a doctoral degree in Neuroimaging. I am a rock climber, cyclist, (whole) foodie, fan of diversity, and lover of travel, adventure, and anything involving the great outdoors.
