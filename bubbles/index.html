Neuroscience of Stories:
 
Stories are more powerful than bullet points.

Bullets activate languate areas of the brain.

Stories activate language areas PLUS areas associated with emotions and experiencing.

A story induces a form of neural mirroring between brains of the speaker and the listener. Hasson* @ Princeton.
