Neuroscience of Stories:
 
Stories are more powerful than bullet points.

Bullet points activate language areas of the brain.

Stories activate language areas PLUS areas associated with emotions and "experiencing"

A story induces a form of neural mirroring between brains of the speaker and the listener. (Princeton)

*

The importance of stories in effective presentations and leadership is documented in literature including HBR.
